About the Gift Shop

The Cathy Griffin Regimental Gift Shop is the official source for Chemical Corps merchandise. Named in honor of Cathy Griffin, whose dedicated service to the CCRA and the Dragon Soldier community helped build and sustain the association's programs.

Every purchase supports the CCRA's mission — funding scholarships, recognition programs, and initiatives that strengthen the Chemical Corps Regiment and its families.
