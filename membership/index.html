Membership

Join the Dragon Soldiers

Become a member of the Chemical Corps Regimental Association and join a proud community dedicated to preserving the heritage and supporting the future of the Chemical Corps.

Who Can Join

Eligibility

Membership in the CCRA is open to Active, Reserve Component, and National Guard service members; Retired service members; present and former Government civilian employees; veterans; cadets of the Service Academies and ROTC; and friends and family of the Chemical Corps.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Annual membership requires yearly renewal at $25. Lifetime membership is a one-time payment ($99–$150 depending on rank) that gives you permanent membership with no renewals. Most members choose Lifetime for the long-term value and to show enduring commitment to the Dragon Soldier family.
