Our Location

The CCRA is headquartered at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri — home of the U.S. Army Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear School (USACBRNS). Fort Leonard Wood serves as the Maneuver Support Center of Excellence and is the training hub for CBRN warriors.
