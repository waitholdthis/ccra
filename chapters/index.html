Start a Local Chapter

Interested in starting a CCRA chapter in your area? Begin by reading the Chapter Constitution and Bylaws section of the Local Chapter Establishment Policy. Your local chapter will fall under Private Organization regulations if located on a military installation.

Approval must be obtained from the local Installation Commander. Once approved, send a formal request to the CCRA national office to obtain a Chapter Charter.
